Connection to a real or virtual Serial Port in your PC.

Configure Port Name and click "Open" button to connect to that serial port.
"Port Name" must be an existing port in your PC with read/write permissions.

Property: (default value)

Main:
- Auto Open: (no)
   Open port automatically at Simulation start.

- Port Name: ()
   Name of the Serial Port to connect to.

Config:
- Baudrate: (9600)
   Transmission speed.

- Data Bits: (8)
   Number of data bits.

- Stop Bits: (1)
   Number of stop bits.
