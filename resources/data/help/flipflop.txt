- Set/Reset Inverted: (yes)
   Invert Set and Reset pins.

- Clock Inverted: (no)
   Invert Clock pin.

- Trigger Type: (Clock)
   "Clock" triggers every active edge.
   "enable" any change during active state.
   "None" hides Clock pin.
