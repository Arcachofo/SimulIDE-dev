Serial terminal to communicate with devices in the simulation.

Connect Rx and Tx Pins to Tx and Rx from a device.
Click "Open" button to open terminal.

Property: (default value)

- Baudrate: (9600)
   Transmission speed.

- Data Bits: (8)
   Number of data bits.

- Stop Bits: (1)
   Number of stop bits.
